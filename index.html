Macro-Systems


Macro-systems are systems that have more than one micro-system built into them. This gives them more functionalities and capabilities to complete more tasks. To put it in simple terms, Macro-systems are multi-tasking systems that can do more tasks, either simultaniously or individually.

An example of a Macro-System would be our agent AFA(located on your right). AFA is built to perform a range of tasks while taking care of any queries you might have.

Here at AssetFlow Automations, we specialise in making Macro-Systems that perform different roles from the admin sector. We use advanched technology to build systems that carry out tasks that require cross-posting on external platforms; such as Google Sheets and Google Docks.
